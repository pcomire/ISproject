A Cross Country Road Trip

Seeing the wonders of the world, mile by mile

Posted by Paige Comire on January 3, 2024

From California to Florida and Back

Back when I was 10 years old, my family of 5 and I adventured on a three-week journey. Driving and visiting all the sights from California to Florida and back.

We saw the dry plains of Arizona, to the bug-infested bathrooms of Texas. From getting rained out at Epcot, and more.

There is much to be said about spending hours on end in a blue mini van with your family. I only remember the good memories.

States We Adventured To

  • California
  • Arizona
  • New Mexico
  • Texas
  • Louisiana
  • Arkansas
  • Tennessee
  • Mississippi
  • Alabama
  • Georgia
  • Florida

The Final Destination - Harry Potter World

After my family and I had been cooped up in a car together on and off for a 10 days, we finally reached our destination - Harry Potter World Orlando. We rode all the rides, drank loads of frozen buterbeer, and got rained out on. All a wonderful memory!
